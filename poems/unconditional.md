---
title: Unconditional
date: '2019-07-22'
---

Simple, yet complex,
the voices in my head
scream a powerful verse,
silently.

Holding on to a
trught I lost my grip.
Butter yet sweet.
A sensation.

An emotion, indescribable,
left me speechless.
Be the words I long for.

My world, and alphabet,
Its intensity?
