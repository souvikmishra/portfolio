---
title: Experience
date: '2019-03-01'
---

I buckled myself
for the adventure
that was about to
unveil itself.
Mystery awaited,
masked by expectations.

Silent.
I could hear voices,
dipped in joy and excitement.
My inclination towards
what I couldn't hear.

Such abstract moments.
Inducing the essence in me
which had vaporized
long ago?
