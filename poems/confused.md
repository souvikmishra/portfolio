---
title: Uncertain
date: '2024-03-07'
---

I know how I walk,
I cannot explain how.
I know how I think,
I cannot explain how.
I know I feel hungry,
I cannot explain how.
I know that I am alive,
I cannot explain how.
I know that I know,
I cannot explain how.
I know I cannot explain how,
I don't know why.
