---
title: Beginning
date: '2019-01-29'
---

Painfully numb,
my soul screamed
for your hand,
you refused to hear.
A bullet would've
calmed me, when you wouldn't.

Scary,
the emptiness of my thoughts,
held a force to powerful,
it took over me.

A storm as elegant it was,
demolished.
The path chosen led to
an inevitable ending,
inevitably...
